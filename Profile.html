Research Profile

Berne Ting

Music Therapy • Brain Science • Evidence-Based Medicine

My research focuses on non-pharmacological interventions for mental health and cognitive well-being, particularly music-based approaches in aging populations. I am also interested in the intersection between generative AI, research methodology, and interdisciplinary communication in medicine and the humanities.
